What is it?


HopOp is a personalised Mobile and Web based Application developed by RedBeak technologies which will help to plan your daily Commute in a better way. The HopOp’s technology basically integrates the existing public transportation system with the App and optimizes it by making the public transport more connective, convenient and economic for the Users.

Our team is consistently working on building the technologies and mean while we have planned to start an alpha Share-Auto Service for Students and Staff of Amrita Vishwavidyapeetam who live in and around Hosa road and Kaikondrahalli areas.

What makes us better than the usual Share-Auto service?


# Door step Pickup and Drop Service
# No.of Users per Auto will not be more than 3
# Dedicated Service only for Student and Staff of Amrita
# Flexible and Economical payment options


How it works?


1. Enrol yourself for a free first ride from the registration form.
2. Our team will get in touch with you and plan your Commute to Amrita
