Our Products

       Here are the verticals our team is consistently working on!!!!



HopOp

IOT based solution for Daily Commute

Our HopOp platform basically aggregates and connects the existing inventory which is used for daily commuting purposes. Based on the commuters need and the data statistics of inventories from our platform, transportation will be managed ensuring Hassle free Commuting, Proper connectivity and Location Tracking with better arrival time forecast. HopOp ensures simple, secure, interactive and economical travel solution for daily commuting people through Android, iOS and Web applications.

HopOp

For Corporate

HopOp Corporate is a clone of HopOp solution. It works for corporate employees who use office transportation for commuting. Our solution connects all subscripted corporate inventories in one platform. With our Statistical Data and Corporate Needs, the inventories will be scheduled ensuring employee transportation with a better traceability , arrival time predictability and economy.

HopOp

Travel Analytics

A subscription based platform which uses data sets collected from Inventories, Customers and day to day Business to perform analysis on the data and provide a better insight of,
1. Optimize utilization of the existing inventories
2. Daily Cost Analysis
3. Customer Behaviour
This helps us to plan and analyze better so that we can give better solutions eventually.
